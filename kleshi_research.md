# SEO Research and Content Plan for "Обработка от клещей" (kleshi.html)

## I. Key SEO Queries and Conversational Phrases (Based on Search Results)

The primary commercial intent queries are:
1.  `акарицидная обработка Омск цена` (Acaricidal treatment Omsk price)
2.  `обработка от клещей на участке Омск` (Tick treatment on a plot Omsk)
3.  `противоклещевая обработка дачи цена` (Anti-tick treatment for dacha price)
4.  `СЭС обработка от клещей Омск` (SES tick treatment Omsk)

Conversational/Informational queries to include (LSI):
*   `как выбрать службу по обработке от клещей` (how to choose a tick treatment service)
*   `сколько стоит обработка от клещей за сотку` (how much does tick treatment cost per hundred square meters)
*   `когда лучше делать обработку от клещей` (when is the best time to do tick treatment)
*   `безопасна ли обработка от клещей для детей и животных` (is tick treatment safe for children and animals)
*   `чем обрабатывают от клещей на участке` (what is used to treat ticks on a plot)

## II. Competitor Analysis and Value Proposition

Competitors (e.g., Главдезцентр, ДЕЗ-Комфорт) are focusing on:
*   Price per hundred square meters (sotka).
*   Guarantees (up to 1 year mentioned).
*   Certified means/preparations.

**"СибДез" Unique Selling Points (USPs) to emphasize:**
1.  **Guarantee:** "Гарантия на весь сезон" (Guarantee for the entire season) - as per the content plan. This is a strong differentiator against 1-year guarantees.
2.  **Safety:** Certified, safe preparations.
3.  **Experience:** 15+ years on the market (from the template).
4.  **Price Transparency:** Clear pricing table (price per `сотка`).

## III. Content Structure (Following `content_plan.md`)

**H1:** Акарицидная обработка от клещей в Омске с гарантией на весь сезон. Цена за сотку.

**1. Введение (Introduction):**
*   The danger of ticks (encephalitis, borreliosis).
*   Why professional treatment is necessary.
*   Call to action: Protect your family and pets.

**2. Преимущества "СибДез" (USPs):**
*   Гарантия на весь сезон (main focus).
*   Безопасные, сертифицированные препараты.
*   Опыт 15+ лет.

**3. Методы и Препараты (Methods and Preparations):**
*   **Метод:** Опрыскивание (холодный туман/мотоопрыскиватель - need to confirm the method). Assume professional spraying with a motor sprayer for large areas.
*   **Препараты:** Mention the use of modern, effective, and safe acaricides (e.g., pyrethroids, organophosphates) without naming specific brands unless they are a strong selling point. Emphasize that they are safe for people and pets after drying.

**4. Цены (Pricing Table):**
*   Must be clear and based on area (`сотка`).
*   Need to use placeholder prices and instruct the user to update them.

**5. Подготовка Участка (Site Preparation):**
*   Mow the grass.
*   Remove debris.
*   Cover children's toys, garden furniture, and pet bowls.

**6. FAQ (5-7 questions):**
*   When is the best time for treatment? (April-May).
*   How long does the effect last? (The whole season).
*   Is it safe for pets/children? (Yes, after 3 hours).
*   What is the price per hundred square meters? (Refer to the table).
*   What to do after treatment? (Wait 3 hours, water the plants).

## IV. Image Plan

*   **Cover Image:** A safe, pleasant image of a family or children enjoying a clean, green lawn/dacha. **NO INSECTS.**
*   **In-Content Image 1:** A professional technician in protective gear using a motor sprayer on a garden plot. (Emphasizes professionalism).
*   **In-Content Image 2:** A visual representation of the guarantee (e.g., a badge or certificate).
